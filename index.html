About

Tate Johnson is a software engineer at LIFX who has a knack for seeing how everything fits together. Simplicity and defaults are my guiding principles. These days I live in Brisbane and commute regularly to Melbourne.

Some of my own software includes:

  • Antenna Mate, an iOS app that takes the guess work out of pointing a TV antenna.
  • Lighting, A Mac OS X widget for toggling LIFX lights on and off.
  • Maps for Antenna Mate, browse TV transmitters over Google Maps from your web browser.
  • Next Stop, an iOS app that alerts you when its time to hop off the bus.
  • Tug, hosted continuous deployment from GitHub to Heroku.
  • Various open source projects and contributions on GitHub.

You can reach me via email (tate@tatey.com) or Twitter (@tatejohnson).
